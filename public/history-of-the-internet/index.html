The Internet

In the early days, most people use Internet solely for searching information. However, as time goes by, it is now used for connecting with people, interacting, accessing files, and even for marketing purposes and e-commerce. It has created big changes to how things work today. From writing letters and sending it through post-offices, and waiting for days or even weeks for a reply, exchange of even a simple "Hi" to someone could happen in just a blink of an eye. From going to shops and physically buying garments, cds, and even appliances and vehicles, one could now purchase online.

Almost everything that we do can be connected to the Internet. We have cellphones, ipad, laptops, and cameras which has the ability to exchange data with the manufacturer and other connected devices locally and internationally, in short Internet of Things. To have a full understanding how these things work and happen, how it all started, why it was even invented, and to have a grasp of the genesis of it, the pages (which could be found by clicking the links above) will somehow explain details about it. Knowing these things will help us understand where we are now and where we're going.
